HELP THE MANATEES
ABOUT SOLUTIONS
BE ALERT

While boating, always obey posted speed zone waterway signs. Remember manatees could be anywhere.

STAYOUT

When in the water, stay at the surface, at a distance, and never approach a resting or feeding manatee. Stay out of posted manatee sanctuary areas. Do not touch, poke or chase manatees in the wild.
